## App Description (Full)

Organize your life with Tasks Management - the intuitive to-do list app that helps you stay on top of your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly tasks.

**Key Features:**

✅ **Multiple List Types** - Organize tasks by Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly, or create Custom lists
✅ **Smart Reminders** - Set multiple reminders for important tasks so you never miss a deadline
✅ **Sub-tasks (Steps)** - Break down complex tasks into manageable steps
✅ **Task Management** - Mark tasks as complete, set due dates, and organize by priority
✅ **List Sharing** - Share your lists with family, friends, or colleagues for collaborative planning
✅ **Search & Filter** - Quickly find tasks with powerful search and filtering options
✅ **Bilingual Support** - Available in English and Hebrew
✅ **Push Notifications** - Get timely reminders delivered directly to your device
✅ **Clean Interface** - Beautiful, intuitive design that makes task management enjoyable

Whether you're managing personal goals, work projects, or family schedules, Tasks Management helps you stay organized and productive.

**Perfect for:**
- Personal task tracking
- Team collaboration
- Project management
- Daily planning
- Goal setting
- Reminder management

Download Tasks Management today and take control of your tasks!
